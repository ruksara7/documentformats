Half Day Leave Application for School

Sometimes students need to leave school early due to illness, personal work, or family reasons. In such situations, students should submit a half day leave application to inform the school authorities.

Below is a simple half day leave application format for school students. The application is provided in both English and Hindi so that students can easily copy and submit it.

How to Write Half Day Leave Application

Address the application to the principal or class teacher. Mention the reason for requesting half day leave. Clearly state that you need leave for half of the school day. Mention the date. End politely and write your name, class and roll number.

Half Day Leave Application (English)

To The Principal [School Name] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Application for Half Day Leave Respected Sir/Madam, I am a student of class ______ in your school. Due to personal reasons, I request permission to leave school early today. Therefore, I kindly request you to grant me half day leave. Thank you. Yours sincerely Name: Class: Roll Number:

FAQ

When should a student write a half day leave application? Students write this application when they need to leave school before the school day ends. Who should the application be addressed to? It should be addressed to the school principal or class teacher. Is half day leave allowed in school? Yes, students can request half day leave with a proper application and permission.
