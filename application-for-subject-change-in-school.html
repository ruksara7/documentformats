Application for Subject Change in School

Sometimes students may need to change a subject due to academic difficulty or interest in another subject. Below are simple copy-ready application formats in English and Hindi.

English Application

To The Principal [School Name] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Application for Subject Change Respected Sir/Madam, I am a student of class ______ at your school. I would like to request a change of my subject from ______ to ______ because ______. Therefore, I kindly request you to allow me to change my subject. Thank you. Yours sincerely Name: Class: Roll Number:

Hindi Application

सेवा में, प्रधानाचार्य महोदय / महोदया [विद्यालय का नाम] दिनांक: ___ / ___ / 20__ विषय: विषय परिवर्तन हेतु आवेदन महोदय / महोदया, मैं आपके विद्यालय की कक्षा ______ का छात्र / छात्रा हूँ। मैं अपना विषय ______ से ______ में बदलना चाहता / चाहती हूँ क्योंकि ______। अतः आपसे विनम्र निवेदन है कि कृपया मेरा विषय परिवर्तन करने की कृपा करें। धन्यवाद। भवदीय नाम: कक्षा: रोल नंबर:

Common Reasons for Subject Change

  • Difficulty understanding the subject
  • Interest in another subject
  • Career planning
  • Recommendation from teachers or parents

Quick Tips

  • Write the current subject clearly.
  • Mention the subject you want to change to.
  • Provide a simple reason.
