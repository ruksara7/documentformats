Application for Provisional Certificate

Students need a provisional certificate after completing their course when the original certificate is not yet issued. Below are simple **copy-ready provisional certificate applications in English and Hindi**.

English Application

To The Principal [School / College Name] [City] Date: ___ / ___ / 20__ Subject: Application for Provisional Certificate Respected Sir/Madam, I am a student of class/course ______ in your institution. I have successfully completed my course and require a provisional certificate for further admission/job purposes. Kindly issue my provisional certificate at the earliest. Thank you. Yours sincerely Name: Class / Course: Roll Number: Contact Number:

Hindi Application

सेवा में, प्रधानाचार्य महोदय / महोदया [विद्यालय / कॉलेज का नाम] दिनांक: ___ / ___ / 20__ विषय: प्रोविजनल प्रमाणपत्र हेतु आवेदन महोदय / महोदया, मैं आपके संस्थान की कक्षा / कोर्स ______ का छात्र / छात्रा हूँ। मैंने अपना पाठ्यक्रम पूर्ण कर लिया है और आगे की पढ़ाई / नौकरी के लिए मुझे प्रोविजनल प्रमाणपत्र की आवश्यकता है। कृपया मुझे प्रोविजनल प्रमाणपत्र जारी करने की कृपा करें। धन्यवाद। भवदीय नाम: कक्षा / कोर्स: रोल नंबर: मोबाइल नंबर:

Documents Required

  • Application letter
  • Student ID card
  • Fee clearance receipt
  • Previous marksheet copy

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a provisional certificate? A temporary certificate issued until the original certificate is provided.
  • Who issues it? School, college or university.
  • How long does it take? Usually 3–7 working days.
